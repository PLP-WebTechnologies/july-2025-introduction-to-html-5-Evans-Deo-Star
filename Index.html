Introduction - Who Are We?

We are a Kenyan initiative that empowers girls and young women to reach their full potential through education, mentorship, and community engagement. Currently we are based in Migori County.

Our Mission

To provide resources, support, and opportunities for girls and young women to thrive, lead, and create positive change in their communities.

Our Vision

A world where every girl and young woman in Kenya is empowered, educated, and equipped to shape her future and contribute meaningfully to society.

Our Core Values

  • Empowerment
  • Education
  • Community
  • Integrity
  • Innovation

Founders

This non-profit organization was founded by Evans Odhiambo Onyango and his team of dedicated volunteers.
